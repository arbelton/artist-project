WHY EXO

Why is EXO awesome?

Their music is unique. They combine so many different genres of music whether it is R&B, ballad, hip-hop, dance etc. They are never afraid to always try different types of music and thus show their diversity and confidence!

Each and every member is an asset to the group. They have not only got unique vocals (most people know EXO as great vocalists but they are so much more), they have got their own style of rap, dance and of course top class visuals. The boys too have great personalities.

They have their own discography and style which appeals to many people and they provide quality content to their fans in all ways and never disappoint!
